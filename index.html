THE SPARK TO CHANGE THE FUTURE

Hello and welcome to Agathians Shelter’s brand new site. The decision to move to a more blog oriented site was to provide us with the flexibility to churn out more updates periodically on what’s going on at the orphanage. Furthermore, this also allows us to extend the site to other volunteers to write on their account events that took place at the orphanage. With that said, we extend our warmest welcomes and are excited to see how things unfold over the next few weeks. Expect more updates and changes as time goes by as we finalize the migration from the old site to this new one.
